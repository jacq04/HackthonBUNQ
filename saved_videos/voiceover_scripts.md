[18–24s]
Money isn't the problem.
It's timing.
We need it exactly when we need it.

[24–38s]
There's a solution that's been working for over 500 years.
It's called a ROSCA — a Rotating Savings and Credit Association.
A group pools money every month. One person takes the full pot when they need it.
No interest. No credit score. Just community.
50 million people still use ROSCAs today. The system works. It always has.

[38–47s]
We took that 500-year-old idea and gave it a backbone.
This is POD.

[47–63s]
Here's how it works.
You set a goal amount and a duration.
POD uses your trust score to match you with a circle of people with similar goals.
Every month, each member's contribution is automatically collected into the pot.
And when you need it most — you call the pot.
The full amount lands in your account, instantly.

[63–75s]
You can experience POD three ways.
The Control Room for admins to manage pods and members in real time.
The web app, where an agentic flow matches you to the right circle based on your goal.
And the mobile app — your pot, your circle, your payments, in your pocket — 24/7.
